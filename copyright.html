About the SIL Open Font License

The SIL Open Font License (OFL) is a free, libre, and open source license specifically designed for fonts. It allows the fonts to be used, studied, modified, and redistributed freely as long as they are not sold by themselves. The fonts used on this site are obtained via Google Fonts and are used in accordance with the OFL.
